# Sprint 1 - Business Simulation Engine

## Objective
Create a realistic e-commerce dataset that simulates customer purchasing behavior.

## Deliverables

- Customer Segmentation
- Product Generation
- Order Generation
- Payment Simulation
- Shipment Simulation
- Returns Simulation
- Reviews Simulation

## Business Rules

### Customer Behaviour

Regular
- 1–3 orders/year
- Average Order Value: ₹700–₹2,000

Silver
- 4–8 orders/year
- Average Order Value: ₹1,500–₹5,000

Gold
- 9–15 orders/year
- Average Order Value: ₹5,000–₹15,000

Platinum
- 20–35 orders/year
- Average Order Value: ₹10,000–₹50,000

### Festival Sales

January - Republic Sale
March - Holi Sale
August - Independence Sale
October - Big Billion Days
November - Diwali Sale
December - Year End Sale

### Payment Preference

UPI - 45%

Credit Card - 25%

Debit Card - 15%

Net Banking - 8%

Cash on Delivery - 7%
