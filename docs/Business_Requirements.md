# NovaCart AI - Intelligent Business Analytics Platform

Version: 1.0

Author: Reshma Kumari

Date: July 2026

---

# 1. Project Overview

NovaCart AI is an AI-powered Business Intelligence platform designed for a simulated e-commerce company. The platform enables executives and business teams to monitor sales, customers, inventory, logistics, returns, and marketing performance using advanced analytics and Artificial Intelligence.

The project combines SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and AI technologies to transform raw business data into meaningful insights and strategic recommendations.

---

# 2. Business Problem

Although NovaCart has experienced continuous growth in sales, the company faces several operational and business challenges.

Current issues include:

- Declining profit margins
- High product return rates
- Customer churn
- Inventory shortages
- Overstocked warehouses
- Shipping delays
- Low marketing ROI
- Lack of centralized reporting

Management currently spends significant time creating reports manually, making it difficult to take timely business decisions.

---

# 3. Project Goal

Develop a centralized Business Intelligence platform capable of:

- Monitoring company performance
- Providing real-time business insights
- Predicting future business trends
- Detecting operational risks
- Supporting data-driven decision making

---

# 4. Stakeholders

- Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- Sales Manager
- Marketing Manager
- Inventory Manager
- Operations Manager
- Customer Support Team
- Data Analytics Team

---

# 5. Project Objectives

The platform should help NovaCart:

- Increase profit margins
- Improve customer retention
- Reduce product return rates
- Optimize inventory
- Improve delivery performance
- Increase marketing effectiveness
- Support executive decision making
